Now that Harper Storm has woken up, tied up in a basement with a madman, she is forced to find out. Face to face with her own failure, Harper has to figure out the best and safest way for her to not only get out alive but to get out with the girls at her side.

---

Harper took a long deep breath as she pulled against the restraints. She kept kicking herself inside, knowing it was her own damn fault for being here, trapped. She shook her head and turned her attention to the two girls who were huddled in the corner, tied up just like her. Their wide eyes and bruised bodies were indications enough for her that they were not going to fight unless they had a clear chance out of there.

She pushed herself up so that she could at least sit straight.

"How long till he normally comes back?" Harper whispered.

"Hour, maybe two," one of the girls whispered back.

"Sh, Sarah," the other girl said. "He doesn't like it if we talk."

Harper nodded. She had an hour to get out of these restraints and figure a way out. If this madman, Hector, returned before then, then she would find herself strangled and buried in the woods. These two girls, Sarah and her sister, were still part of his plan. Harper, who strayed in to ask some questions and walked right into his trap, well she was not.

Harper took her time to look around the dimly lit room. There was a light bulb, flashing and swinging to the draft of the wind. She noticed the wooden boards that barred the one exit on the side of the room. She got on her knees and crawled closer. Sure enough, there was a protruding nail. It was a sloppy job and one that might just be the key to her escape.

Harper knew her Yoga classes might finally come in handy. She grunted, shifting her weight. She took a deep breath as she heaved herself forward. She pushed with her legs till she finally stood straight. Harper grunted as she bent down, her chest pressing against her legs as her wrists reached her feet. She lowered herself again until her back hit the floor. She bent her legs and her arms finally slipped over her feet.

She gave a big sigh of relief as she headed towards the wood boards and nails. This was a tough, coarse rope, but with friction, she should be able to get herself free. She started hacking her arms up and down against the protruding nail, making sure the ropes started to fray little by little. She kept going, and soon she could feel the ropes getting weaker.

A slam above them caused Harper to freeze for a second. She heard Sarah's whimpers, and her heartbeat spiked. She jerked against the ropes. It was still a little too strong. She returned to hacking it against the nail, as steady creaks resonated on the floor.

She tried snapping the ropes apart again, but still, no results. She kept hacking at it as she heard the creaks turning into steady thuds as Hector was now making his way down the stairs.

She tried snapping it again. Her eyes widened as the ropes finally gave way, freeing her hands. She turned her attention to her feet but noticed that the knots were far too strong.

She heard a small squeal again and turned to see the door opening. She fell back down, her back facing the door and her hands hidden.

"There there, right where I left you," a soft voice cooed behind her.

A moment of silence made Harper swallow.

"Not where I left you."

One footstep. Another footstep.

A strong hand gripped her shoulder. Harper gritted her teeth. She swung her arms and her fists struck his face. He fell back. Harper felt like a feral cat as she jumped onto his chest and pressed her fingers against his eyes. He screamed, and with his arm struck her side. She fell back, her head striking the wall. She looked around, completely dazed and watched as Hector staggered up. His hand was on his one bleeding eye.

"I'm gonna kill you!" he screamed as he stormed towards her.

Harper kicked out, aiming for his knees. It made contact and he fell back. She heard a clatter and noticed the shining blade flashing in the light. She crawled for it, snatching it up and started to cut the ropes on her legs as Hector was dazed and trying to regain his composure.

As soon as her legs were free she jumped up, only to be grabbed by the hair and tossed to the side. The girls kept screaming, and even Harper gave out a cry in pain. But she still had a grip on the knife, and she knew she was going to have to use it.
